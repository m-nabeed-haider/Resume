# FakeLess

## Overview

FakeLess is a deep learning research project focused on protecting speech recordings from unauthorized AI voice cloning.

The project generates carefully optimized perturbations that preserve perceptual audio quality while reducing the ability of modern neural voice cloning systems to reproduce a speaker's identity.

---

## Objectives

- Prevent unauthorized voice cloning
- Preserve natural speech quality
- Train end-to-end deep learning models
- Evaluate robustness against speaker verification systems
